Kilopi Documentation

Welcome to The Kilopi's official documentation and help resource. Please navigate trough the left menu and send an email to info@kilopi.net if you think something is missing.

What is Kilopi

Kilopi is an ecosystem of web3 dApps. These dApss are being developed by the team and the community together. There is no limit on dApps' categories. From Games to DAOs, from Polls to Engineering. Many different kind of dApps are being developed at the same time. Kilopi has a very simple forum-alike D.A.O dApp where the community can propose and vote for anything regarding the Kilopi Project’s ecosystem development.

Kilopi Mission

The mission of Kilopi project is to be one of the top token-based projects in the world by having the best sustainable web3 dApps.

Kilopi Vision

The vision of the Kilopi project is to create a decentralized, fully community driven, sustainable consensus system where the community is the real owner of the project and the developers develop the project with enjoy and reasonable benefits without getting disturbed by a central authority.

Kilopi Operation

Kilopi is a place where the community shapes the Kilopi Project’s dynamics by involving the development progress with their proposals and votes. At Kilopi, community is the real owner of the project. Community discusses, proposes, votes and the developers proceed with the results. All operation is decentralized, simple and sustainable.

Kilopi Future

Kilopi team releases new roadmaps every 6 months and do his best to follow the schedule. There are many plans about the future of the Kilopi project. But one thing is for sure; "Building as many as sustainable dApps which have the LOP token as utility"

Kilopi Decentralized Autonomous Organization

Kilopi has one of the best D.A.O App supported with the founder's youtube channel.

Kilopi Pathfinder

Pathfinder is a Poll dApp which creates a real use case for LOP token and guide the community of the project to learn about the industry and benefit from the good developments

Kilopi Collector

Collector is the staking dApplication of Kilopi project. There are 2 main options as locked and non-locked stakings. There are 3 alternatives under the locked staking system as well.

Kilopi No Doubt

No Doubt is the unique transparency web3 dApp that shows all the token moves of the project on chain.

Kilopi Masters of Management

Masters of Management is a unique decentralized turn-based strategy game that has the LOP token as utility.

Kilopi Skallia UO Shard

Skallia is a unique Ultima Online Shard that has the LOP token as utility.

Kilopi Smart Engineering Solutions

Smart Engineering Solutions are small helper dApps for engineers, architects and more. LOP token is the main utility token of these smart dApps.

Kilopi Matcher Platform

Matcher is a unique platform that matches the clients with contractors in a sustainable and archivable way. LOP token is the main utility token of this platform.
